Cyber Security Trends by Industry

Different industries face unique cybersecurity challenges based on their operations, regulatory environment, and the type of data they handle.

Healthcare

The healthcare industry remains a prime target due to the high value of medical records on the black market. Trends include increased ransomware attacks on hospitals, vulnerabilities in IoT medical devices, and compliance challenges with regulations like HIPAA.

Financial Services

Banks and financial institutions face sophisticated attacks aimed at stealing funds and customer data. Key trends include API security challenges in open banking, AI-powered fraud detection systems, and increasing regulatory scrutiny.

Retail & E-Commerce

With the growth of online shopping, retail faces payment fraud, supply chain attacks, and credential stuffing. Emerging trends include the need for bot mitigation and protecting customer data across multiple touchpoints.

Manufacturing

Industrial IoT and operational technology systems are vulnerable to disruption. Trends include ransomware targeting production lines, intellectual property theft, and the convergence of IT and OT security.

Government

Government agencies face nation-state attacks, election security challenges, and securing legacy systems. Trends include zero trust adoption, securing remote work for government employees, and protecting critical infrastructure.

Education

Schools and universities face ransomware attacks disrupting operations, protecting student data, and securing remote learning platforms. Trends include securing hybrid learning environments and protecting research data.
